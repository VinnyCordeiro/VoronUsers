# Phaetus' Dragon hotend's mold for DIY heatbreak socks

Took the inspiration from pyr0ball and Stefan, from CNC Kitchen: https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:2848281, https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:3030379, and https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:3742037. I highly recommend watching [Stefan's video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=O5UiXky85hA) to understand how it works.

The Dragon hotend, originally sold by Trianglelab at Aliexpress, was released about an year ago and took Voron community by storm; later, a version of the Dragon was released by Phaetus. As far as I understand it, both are manufactured by the same OEM, the only difference between them being the heatblock: while the Trianglelab version uses the standard V6 heatblock, Phaetus version have a custom shape. So I went to Phaetus' website, downloaded the reference 3d model of their version of the Dragon, and made this mold for those who want to DIY heatblock socks using RTV silicone.

## Updates:
April 14, 2021: Initial upload. Included STEP files of the mold as well as STL files, which are not resized for plastic shrinkage. You may need to print it at 101% or 102% on your slicer of choice.
